## Plot

A troupe of ballerinas is on the way to a dance competition when their bus breaks down on a quiet road. They take shelter for the night at a remote inn run by Devora (Uma Thurman), a former ballet prodigy. Pretty Lethal (2026) becomes a gentle night-in puzzle where the dancers use their studio skills to stay calm and find a safe way out by morning.
